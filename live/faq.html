Getting Started

Cleo is a postpartum support service for new mothers. We coordinate in-home care, wellness treatments, meal delivery, and mental health support — all through a single concierge. We're for any mother navigating the fourth trimester and wanting expert, personalized care without the chaos of vetting and coordinating herself.

Sign up directly here, reach out to us via email, or book an intro call.

We ask that you book as soon as possible — ideally before the second trimester, but we will work with you on your timeline. The earlier we connect, the more time we have to build the right plan before your baby arrives.

We currently serve the greater Los Angeles area. If you're located outside this region, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs or connect you with trusted resources in your area.

Services & Providers

Every practitioner in our network has been personally vetted for their credentials, experience, and commitment to compassionate maternal care. We only partner with licensed professionals who share our mission of supporting mothers through the fourth trimester. You can read more on our Our Team page.

We offer postpartum doula support, lactation consulting, chef-prepared meal delivery, massage therapy, acupuncture, mental health therapy, night care, and home cleaning — all coordinated through your care concierge. See the full list on our Offerings page.

Most Cleo services are delivered in-home — doulas, massage therapists, acupuncturists, night care providers, and cleaning teams all come to you. Mental health support and lactation consulting are also available virtually. Your concierge will confirm the format that works best for each service in your plan.

Packages & Pricing

Packages are pre-designed plans based on the number of weeks you would like to use our services and a choice between our Essential or Premium offerings. The latter including in-home postpartum doula support. Both package options include daily meal delivery, mental health support, wellness treatments and our Welcome Home Kit. Individual bookings are an option for families that would like to choose specific offerings. For both options, booking will be through our bespoke concierge service so that they can handle the scheduling directly with practitioners. All communication will be streamlined through one point of contact.

Absolutely. Every plan we build is bespoke. Our sample packages are a starting point — your concierge will adjust frequency, services, and duration based on your specific needs, preferences, and budget.

Accepting insurance is dependent on the practitioners. Some work with insurance and we will help you navigate that process.

Support & Mental Health

We connect mothers with licensed perinatal mental health specialists for therapy, postpartum depression screening, anxiety support, and ongoing check-ins. Sessions can be virtual or in-person. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact your healthcare provider or call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) immediately.

Cleo isn't only for mothers in crisis — it's for any mother who wants the fourth trimester to feel supported, not overwhelming. Whether you're navigating a difficult recovery or simply want the logistics handled so you can focus on your baby, we're here for you. A supported recovery is a form of self-love.
