Enter Cleo

A concierge postpartum service. Vetting, matching and coordinating everything you need to recover fully, at home.

The American medical system treats birth as an ending. Cleo recognises it as a beginning.

Cleo isn't just a platform. It's your well-connected confidante who deeply understands the healthcare system, does the legwork, and coordinates every detail.

Our Process

Each family's postpartum journey is unique, but the need for support is universal. We believe you shouldn't have to navigate this alone, and we built Cleo so you don't have to.

Through our packages or individual booking options, we craft an integrated recovery program vetted by experts and tailored specifically to your needs. We take care of the details, so you can focus on what matters most.

As your advocate, we also help you to navigate the complex insurance system and make sure each family understands their benefits.

Three Pillars of Recovery

While all aspects of healing are deeply connected, we approach our offerings through three interconnected pillars, empowering you to thrive postpartum.

Physical

Your body has done something extraordinary. Healing it takes more than rest. It takes intentional, expert care. We coordinate targeted bodywork, massage, and acupuncture alongside chef-prepared meals designed specifically for postpartum recovery, all delivered to your door.

Emotional

The fourth trimester is as much an emotional and mental shift as a physical one. Night care gives you the rest you need to feel like yourself again, while licensed mental health therapists ensure the weight of new motherhood is never carried alone.

Connection

Confidence is part of recovery. Our lactation consultants and postpartum doulas are there not just to support, but to guide, helping you understand your body, feed your baby, and feel truly equipped for early motherhood.
