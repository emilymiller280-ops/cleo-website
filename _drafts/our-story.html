Our Story

Born from a belief that mothers deserve better.

Although birth is arguably the most natural experience of a woman's life, our society is not set up to support her journey into motherhood. With the rapid rise of perinatal mental health disorders and misinformation on social media, it's clear we can do better.

The Name

Cleo — from the Greek kleos, to praise and to celebrate.

We chose it intentionally. Because we believe every mother deserves to be celebrated — not just for the miracle of birth, but for the courage, endurance, and love that defines the fourth trimester and beyond.

Our Mission

To transform the postpartum experience by providing comprehensive, vetted support that empowers mothers to focus on recovery and connection with their newborns.

We believe every mother deserves access to professional care that honors the sacred fourth trimester.

A Note from Our Founder

I watched friends and family piece together their postpartum plans through fragmented mom groups, Instagram reels, and word of mouth. It was exhausting — and it didn't have to be.

You've navigated fertility challenges, nine months of carrying, and the extraordinary act of delivery. You deserve to hand off the coordinating of these seminal weeks so that you can rest and focus on your growing family.

Cleo exists to make that possible — to simplify this chapter through vetted expert guidance and support, all in one place.

Emily Miller

Founder, Cleo

What We Believe

The values that guide everything we do.

Support is Self-Love

A supported recovery is a form of self-love and love for your baby. Asking for help is strength, not weakness.

Every Mother is Different

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to postpartum. We celebrate the unique needs of each family and create personalized care plans.

Education & Connection

Knowledge is power. We connect mothers with expert guidance and a community of support to navigate this transformative time.
