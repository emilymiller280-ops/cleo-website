The Promise

The most demanding chapter of motherhood, taken care of.

The weeks after birth place real nutritional demands on every new mother's body — recovery, sleep loss, and, for those who breastfeed, milk production — at exactly the moment she has the least time to cook for herself.

Cleo Care is chef-prepared meal delivery built specifically for postpartum recovery. Every meal is designed to restore a depleted body. And for mothers who breastfeed, each one is also screened against what the science says doesn't belong on the plate during this window.

However you choose to feed your baby, the recovery nutrition is the same — no planning, no second-guessing, just real, restorative food, delivered.

Why it's different

Cleo Care optimizes for this season of a woman's life.

We started from our signature chef-driven kitchen and added a layer that doesn't exist anywhere else — a postpartum & breastfeeding safety classification, applied to every ingredient before it reaches the menu. It isn't only about what we leave out. What we put in matters just as much.

The Cleo Care Classification

Recovery nutrition is the foundation of every Cleo Care plate, whether or not you breastfeed. For mothers who do, we add one more layer: working from authoritative medical guidance, we reviewed our entire 1,000+ ingredient library and sorted every item into a clear, evidence-based framework — so you don't have to be the expert.

Exclude

Ingredients with solid evidence against use while breastfeeding. These never appear on a Cleo Care menu.

Limit

Safe in moderation. We control portion and frequency so they stay within recognized guidance.

Use with care

Fine in normal culinary amounts. We simply avoid building a dish around large or concentrated quantities.

The result is a menu where every plate is already inside the lines.
