Packages vs Individual Bookings

First, choose between curated packages or individual bookings.

Both options are coordinated through our dedicated concierge service — scheduling directly with practitioners, all communication streamlined through one point of contact.

What's the difference?

Packages

With the direction from our board of experts, we've pre-designed plans based on the number of weeks you'd like our support. These offerings combine in-home care, wellness treatments and meal delivery for complete support all in the comfort of your own home.

Individual Bookings

An option for families that would like to choose specific offerings. For example, two weeks of meal delivery or arrange for postpartum support for the first month post delivery.
