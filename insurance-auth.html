Authorization to verify your insurance benefits

This lets Cleo Care contact your insurer to check your coverage on your behalf — and, where it applies, submit claims for you. Please read the two authorizations below, then add your name and agree at the bottom.

This authorization applies to the insurance and member information you already provided to Cleo at sign-up (provider, plan, member ID, group number, date of birth). There's nothing to re-enter here.
1

Authorization to Release Health Information

I authorize Cleo Care, LLC and its designated staff to contact my insurer and to request, receive, and discuss information regarding my insurance coverage and benefits for the purpose of verifying my eligibility and benefits. This may include my plan status, deductible and out-of-pocket amounts, copay and coinsurance details, coverage for specific services, in-network and out-of-network benefits, and any prior authorization requirements.

I authorize the release of information necessary to perform this verification — including my name, date of birth, member identification number, and group number — to my insurer and its representatives.

I understand I may revoke this authorization at any time by submitting a written request to Cleo Care, LLC, and that revocation will not affect actions already taken in reliance on it. I understand that information disclosed under this authorization may no longer be protected by HIPAA once received by my insurer, and that my insurer's own privacy policies will govern its use thereafter.

2

Authorization for Claims Submission

I designate Cleo Care, LLC as my authorized agent for submitting health insurance claims to my insurer on my behalf. I understand and agree that:

  • Cleo will submit claims using superbills and session documentation provided by my treating provider(s).
  • All reimbursements are issued directly to me by my insurer; Cleo will not receive, handle, or redirect any reimbursement funds on my behalf.
  • Cleo acts solely as a submission agent and is not an insurance company, third-party administrator, or claims adjudicator.
  • This authorization does not transfer any insurance benefits or rights to Cleo.
  • I remain responsible for verifying my own coverage, deductibles, and any insurer-specific requirements.
  • I may revoke this authorization at any time in writing; revocation takes effect going forward and does not apply to claims already submitted.
  • Some insurers may require a separate authorization on file directly with them before accepting third-party claim submissions. Cleo will notify me if this applies to my plan.
